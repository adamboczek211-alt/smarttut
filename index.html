SmartTUT

Real-time biceps curl tracker with Time Under Tension analysis. Connect your wristband to begin.

Step 1
Turn on your SmartTUT wristband using the power switch
Step 2
Click "Connect BLE" and select SmartTUT from the list
Step 3
Choose Freestyle or Precision mode and calibrate

Requires Google Chrome with Bluetooth enabled
